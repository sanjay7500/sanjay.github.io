Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I need a professional email address for my business?

Your email address sets the first impression amongst your clients and stakeholders. A professional branded email address builds credibility for you and your business and sets it apart from a generic email id. What's more, it improves collaboration and communication among team members and helps organizes your business too.

Why should I buy Business Email over other email offerings?

You should buy the Business Email product if you are looking for a reliable and easy-to-use email solution that helps you send & receive emails across multiple devices quickly and without hassle. Get this if you don't need collaboration tools for your business, teams or family.

Why should I buy Enterprise Email over other email offerings?

You should by the Enterprise Email product if you are looking for a powerful and feature-packed suite that provides all the benefits of Business Email bundled with productivity tools for collaboration and better work management. If you're looking to expand your group / team / organization / customer base quickly, Enterprise Email is the perfect choice for you.

Why should I buy G Suite over other email offerings?

G Suite is a premium offering from Google that is used by customers and businesses worldwide. It offers the familiar Gmail and Google Drive interface and functionality, all on your custom domain name. If you are looking for a premiere offering for your business requirements, G Suite is for you!

Why do I have to pay for Email Services? Aren't other providers offering this for free?

There are a few problems with using email services from a free email provider:
  • You most probably won't get the name you want. Common names like Anand Shah are already taken and you’ll have to resort to getting lengthy and confusing ones like “[email protected]” to communicate
  • You miss a significant opportunity to build your personal / professional brand Your contacts will find it harder to remember your long and vague email address
  • Your contacts will find it harder to remember your email address
  • You will see ads in your email interface and in the emails that you send out
With BigRock's three Email Offerings, you get professional branding and memorable email addresses '@YOURdomainname.com' and won't see any obtrusive ads on these services.

I have a Domain Name with another provider. Can I use your Email services?

Yes, you can! You will be prompted to enter an existing domain name OR buy a new one from BigRock during the purchase process. On selecting the first option, you can enter your existing domain name to buy email services for it.

What is Email Hosting?

Email hosting allows you to create personalized and professional email addresses for you and your business. You get dedicated storage space for your emails and attachments and can send or receive mails from an intuitive Gmail-like webmail interface.

You also get additional feature-packed collaboration tools like Calendar, Tasks, Documents with some flavors. These can help you work better within your teams, groups and organization.

How much bandwidth and disk Space does the hosting plan include?

Our web hosting plans provide sufficient resources for a personal or small business website. There are no set limits on the bandwidth and disk space for Shared and Reseller Hosting.
However, all customers need to be compliant with our Terms of Service. For eg., Your website should not utilize more than 25% server resources for a duration longer than 90 seconds. Also, the web hosting plan should not be used for file storage or sharing. While it is rare, we might have to suspend your account, if the resource usage is affecting the customers on the same server.

What if I exceed the allotted server usage?

In case you exceed the bandwidth, we will send an email asking you to limit your usage and we might throttle your site’s bandwidth to ensure other users on the system are not affected.
